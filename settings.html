RPC Client

Public RPC is the pre-setup RPC client. It does not have support for pictures. If you want to use pictures go here and click on "New Application" name it (the name will be what it will be called in your "playing ${Name}") click on the "Copy" button under the client ID and paste it in the text box shown when you set the RPC Client to Private RPC. To add images simply click on Rich Presence then Art Assets, then scroll down to Add Image(s) and upload your images. Then take the asset key (the name the image is given after saving) and paste it into one of the image key boxes in the main window.
